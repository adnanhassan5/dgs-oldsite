All About DGIS

Welcome to the story of Dubai Grand International School (DGIS), where every chapter is filled with resilience, innovation, and a commitment to excellence in education. Inaugurated on the auspicious day of November 14, 2015 by Shah Abubakar Ahmed Grand Mufti of India, DGIS embarked on a journey to redefine the educational landscape in Srinagar, India. In pursuance of its motto and its commitment to the prime motive “Success in Both Worlds”.

The early years were marked by challenges, particularly during the unrest in Kashmir from 2016 to 2017, which led to the closure of the school for an entire session. However, adversity only served to strengthen our resolve. In 2017, under the dynamic leadership of Chairman Mr. Mohammad Haneef Bhat who believes that “our survival depends on our ability to stay awake to adjust to the new ideas, to remain vigilant and to face the challenges to change” and rose with renewed vigor and innovation.

Nestled on the picturesque HMT, NH1, Green Avenue, Srinagar, with hardwork and resilience in determination, we DGIS sprawls over 13 acres of land, providing a serence and conducive environment for learning. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities including spacious and well ventilated classrooms, libraries, IT labs, equipped labouratory, sports equipment, play grounds, music room, medical centre and well maintained toilet facilities ensuring a holistic development and experience for our students.

DGIS started from pre-nursery to class 7th in its early years before expanding to include a senior wing. DGIS is more than just a school; it’s a gateway to the world. Since the journey, DGIS organized national and international tours, including Umrah in order to provide our students with opportunities for cultural immersion and global exposure, broadening their horizons and shaping them into well-rounded individuals that aims to create a new and innovative environment for student learning whose prime motive is to accomplish the prime endeavour of making desired changes in the society by preparing students for success in both the worlds.

The senior wing of DGIS stands as a testament to our commitment to academic excellence, boasting advanced physics, chemistry and biology labs along with a workshop centre that fosters hands-on learning and experimentation.

Our dedication to academic excellence is reflected in our outstanding track record, with DGIS achieving a remarkable 100 percent pass rate in 10th grade examinations for the past three years. This achievement is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our faculty, the support of our community and our unwavering commitment to nurturing the potential within each student.

As we look to the future, DGIS remains steadfast in our mission to empower students to become leaders, innovators, and global citizens. We invite you to join us on this extraordinary journey of learning, growth, and transformation at Dubai Grand International School, where every day is an opportunity to excel and inspire. Welcome to DGIS, where greatness begins and dreams take flight.
